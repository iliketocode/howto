Naming a Pet




Here are the steps of making a pet name!


Think of the color/fur pattern of your pet. You could include this is it's name (Goldy, Spotty, Snowy)


You could describe your pet's personality and make that its name (Lazy, Funny)


You could use a normal human name (Joey, Rex)


What type of animal is your pet? Your name could be based on this (Kitty, Fishy, Doggy)
